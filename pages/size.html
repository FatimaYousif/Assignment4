SIZING GUIDE

HOW DO I MEASURE MYSELF?

All measurements are in centimetres (cm) and all products are listed in Australian sizes. Please note: if you are unsure about any sizing, kindly email us here.

Chest: Measured across the garment at the bust.
Waist: Measured across the garment at the narrowest part.
Hip: Measured at the widest part of the garment.
Sleeve Length: From the shoulder seam to the wrist.
Shoulder: Measured from one shoulder seam to the other.

Body Measurements
The below chart outlines body measurements for each Hijab House size. To measure your body, please follow these easy steps:
Chest: Around the fullest part of your chest/bust, under your arms.
Waist: To be measured around the natural waistline. The measuring tape must be horizontal against the body.
Hip: Around the fullest part of the hip. Measuring tape must be horizontal against the body.
Sleeve Length: Measurement from sleeve head (shoulder seam) to wrist.
